Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before booking

What is the distance between the apartment and the beach?
The apartment is located only 200 meters (2-3 minutes walk) from Mermerli beach and 600m from Adalar Beach Club, renowned for their rocky coves and crystal-clear waters. You can easily go back and forth throughout the day.
How many people can stay in the apartment?
The apartment can comfortably accommodate 6 to 8 people. It has 3 bedrooms (one with a double bed and a single sofa bed, two with 2 single beds) and a sofa bed in the living room that can accommodate 1 additional person. Ideal for families or groups of friends.
Is parking available?
There is no reserved parking, but we help you find a spot 200m from the apartment, even in high season.
What is the distance from Antalya airport?
The apartment is located about 12km from Antalya International Airport, approximately 15-20 minutes by car depending on traffic. We can arrange a private airport transfer upon request to make your arrival easier.
Are pets allowed?
Unfortunately, pets are not allowed in the apartment.
What are the check-in and check-out times?
Check-in is from 3:00 PM and check-out until 11:00 AM. Flexible arrangements are possible depending on availability - don't hesitate to contact us if you have specific time constraints.
Is there WiFi and is it fast?
Yes, the apartment has WiFi in all rooms.
Do I need to rent a car to get around?
It is not mandatory but recommended if you want to explore the region. The apartment is well served by public transport (bus/tram stop at 300m), and many shops and restaurants are within walking distance. A car will give you more freedom to visit tourist sites around Antalya.
What is the best time to visit Antalya?
Antalya enjoys a pleasant Mediterranean climate almost year-round. High season is from May to October with warm temperatures (25-35°C). April-May and September-October are perfect for avoiding crowds and discovering surrounding archaeological sites. Winter (November-March) is mild and ideal for exploring the city without intense heat. Comfortable swimming (sea above 20°C - up to 30°C) from early May to Christmas.
What are the cancellation conditions?
Free cancellation up to 30 days before arrival. Between 30 and 14 days: 50% refund. Less than 14 days: no refund. In case of exceptional circumstances (serious illness, travel restrictions), we will study each situation on a case-by-case basis.
